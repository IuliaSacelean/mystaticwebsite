Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ScenariX is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how your data is protected.

Information We Collect

Acceptance Criteria Input: The text you enter into ScenariX is sent securely to our server for the sole purpose of generating test cases. We do not store, sell, or use this text for any other purpose.

Device Information: We do not collect personal identifiers, device IDs, analytics, or tracking data.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Your input text is processed in real time to generate test cases.
  • No user profiles are created.
  • No data is used for advertising or marketing.

Data Storage

ScenariX does not store your acceptance criteria or generated test cases on our servers. All processing is temporary and deleted automatically after generation.

Third‑Party Services

ScenariX does not integrate with third‑party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

Children’s Privacy

ScenariX is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Because ScenariX does not store personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete.

Changes to This Policy
