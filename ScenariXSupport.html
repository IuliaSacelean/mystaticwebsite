ScenariX Support

Welcome to the ScenariX support page. Here you’ll find quick answers, basic guides, and ways to reach us if you get stuck.

Getting started

First steps with ScenariX.

  • Create a new scenario: Open ScenariX and choose “New Scenario”, then paste your acceptance criteria.
  • Generate test cases: Click “Generate Tests” to create structured test cases from your criteria.
  • Edit test cases: Adjust scenarios, steps, and wording directly in the interface.
  • Export: Use the export options (e.g. JSON, CSV, or other formats you’ve enabled) to share or store your tests.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and quick checks.

  • No test cases are generated: Make sure your acceptance criteria are not empty and are written in clear sentences.
  • Export doesn’t work: Check that your browser or device allows downloads and that you have enough storage.
  • Blank or frozen screen: Refresh the page, close and reopen the app, or clear the browser cache.
  • Server or AI errors: Wait a few seconds and try again; if it persists, contact support with a screenshot.

FAQ

  • Do I need internet? Yes, ScenariX needs an internet connection to generate test cases using AI.
  • Can I reuse scenarios? Yes, you can keep, duplicate, and refine existing scenarios as templates.
  • Can I share test cases with my team? You can export them and share via your preferred tools (email, Jira, test management, etc.).
