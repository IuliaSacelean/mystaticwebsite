Spinning Kitty Game – Privacy Policy

Overview

Spinning Kitty Game values your privacy. This policy explains what information the game collects, how it is used, and how your data is protected.

Information We Collect

Spinning Kitty Game is designed to be fun, simple, and safe. The game does not collect personal data unless explicitly stated. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number) is collected.
  • No gameplay data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs are used.
  • Any progress, unlocked cats, or game settings remain stored locally on your device.

How We Use Information

The game only uses local device storage to:

  • Save your unlocked cats and game progress.
  • Remember your in‑game preferences.
  • Ensure the game runs smoothly on your device.

No information is transmitted, shared, or sold.

Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. Because no external servers are used, your information stays private and under your control.

Children’s Privacy

Spinning Kitty Game is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You may delete the game at any time to remove all locally stored data. Since no cloud storage is used, uninstalling the app permanently removes all saved progress.
