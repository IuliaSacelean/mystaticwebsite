Frequently asked questions
Updated
  • How do I add a new contribution?
    Tap Add Contribution, enter the donor’s details, amount, and year, then press Save.
  • Can I export my data?
    Yes, use the Export to CSV option to download your contributions.
  • How do I print a receipt?
    Select a contributor, tap Print Receipt, and choose your preferred destination (PDF, printer, or share).
  • Is my data secure?
    Nova360 uses secure storage and transparent workflows to keep your records safe.
